## Part 8: The Refinement Checklist

Before submitting any prompt, check:

- ☐ **Audience/Context**: Did I say who this is for or why I'm asking?
- ☐ **Structure**: Did I specify format (list, steps, summary, etc.)?
- ☐ **Scope**: Is it focused on ONE thing, not many?
- ☐ **Constraints**: Did I set limits (length, number of items, style)?
- ☐ **Clarity**: Could someone else read this and know exactly what I want?
